ABOUT

Situated in West Belfast, Seven Coffee was founded in 2021 to bring high quality Barista style coffee, tea and food to serve the local community. We grew up and lived in the area and have worked hard to bring the best to the west.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

Check out our social media for up to date information and news

Opening hours: mon-fri from 8am to 4pm, sat-sun from 10am to 4pm.

Address: 7 Glen Road, Belfast BT11 8AE
