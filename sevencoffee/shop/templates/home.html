About

Situated in West Belfast, No 7 Coffee was founded in 2021 to bring high quality Barista style coffee, tea and food to serve the local community. We grew up and lived in the area and have worked hard to bring the best to the west.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff. All sourced from local independent suppliers and to meet all dietary requirements. We also offer catering for small events and delivery and collection of treat boxes, afternoon teas for one or two, picnic hampers and specialty gift boxes.

Opening hours: mon-sat from 7am to 5pm, sun from 10am to 1pm.

Address: 7 Glen Road, Belfast BT11 8AE
